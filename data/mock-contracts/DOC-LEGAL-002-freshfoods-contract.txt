LOGISTICS SERVICE AGREEMENT CTR-2024-002
Between: FreshFoods Vertriebs GmbH (Client) and LogiCore Transport GmbH (Provider)
Effective: 2024-06-01 — 2025-05-31

1. Scope of Services
Refrigerated transport of fresh produce and dairy products across Germany.
- Daily routes: 12 fixed routes covering 180 retail locations
- Temperature range: 2-6 degrees Celsius (fresh) / -18 degrees (frozen)
- Delivery windows: 05:00-09:00 (before store opening)

2. Service Level Agreement
- On-time delivery: >= 97%
- Product quality at delivery: zero visible damage
- Temperature compliance: 100% (continuous monitoring required)
- Vehicle cleanliness audit score: >= 95%

3. Penalty Clauses
3.1 Late delivery (after 09:00): EUR 200 per affected store
3.2 Temperature deviation: EUR 1,000 per incident
3.3 Product damage due to handling: full replacement at wholesale price
3.4 Annual penalty cap: EUR 150,000

4. Pricing
- Monthly base fee: EUR 28,000
- Per-delivery fee: EUR 8.50
- Fuel surcharge: adjusted quarterly based on diesel index
- Estimated annual value: EUR 650,000

5. Seasonal Adjustments
Summer months (June-August): 20% volume increase expected.
December: 35% volume increase for holiday season.
Provider must maintain capacity flexibility of +40% above baseline.
