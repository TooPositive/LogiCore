SERVICE AGREEMENT CTR-2024-001
Between: PharmaCorp AG (Client) and LogiCore Transport GmbH (Provider)
Effective: 2024-03-01 — 2026-02-28

1. Scope of Services
LogiCore Transport shall provide temperature-controlled pharmaceutical logistics services including:
- Warehouse storage at certified GDP (Good Distribution Practice) facilities
- Last-mile delivery to 340 pharmacies across Germany, Austria, and Switzerland
- Real-time temperature monitoring and chain-of-custody documentation

2. Service Level Agreement (SLA)
- On-time delivery rate: >= 98.5%
- Temperature excursion rate: < 0.1% of shipments
- Order processing time: < 4 hours from order receipt
- Delivery window accuracy: +/- 30 minutes

3. Penalty Clauses
3.1 Late delivery: EUR 500 per shipment below 98.5% threshold
3.2 Temperature excursion: EUR 2,000 per incident + full cargo replacement cost
3.3 Repeated SLA breach (3 consecutive months): Client may terminate with 30 days notice
3.4 Maximum annual penalty cap: EUR 500,000

4. Pricing
- Base fee: EUR 45,000 per month
- Per-shipment fee: EUR 12.50 (standard) / EUR 18.00 (cold-chain)
- Warehousing: EUR 3.50 per pallet-day
- Estimated annual contract value: EUR 1,200,000

5. Insurance
LogiCore maintains EUR 10,000,000 cargo insurance.
PharmaCorp requires additional pharmaceutical product liability coverage of EUR 5,000,000.

6. Regulatory Compliance
Both parties comply with EU GDP guidelines, German Arzneimittelgesetz (AMG), and applicable customs regulations for cross-border pharmaceutical transport.
