Driver Safety Protocol
LogiCore Transport GmbH — Operations Department

1. Pre-Trip Inspection
Every driver must complete the 15-point pre-trip inspection checklist before departing. This includes tire pressure, brake check, lights, mirrors, load securing, and refrigeration unit verification for temperature-controlled shipments.

2. Driving Hours Compliance
EU Regulation (EC) No 561/2006 applies to all LogiCore drivers:
- Maximum daily driving: 9 hours (extendable to 10 hours twice per week)
- Maximum weekly driving: 56 hours
- Maximum fortnightly driving: 90 hours
- Continuous driving break: 45 minutes after 4.5 hours

3. Emergency Procedures
In case of accident: secure the scene, call emergency services (112), notify LogiCore dispatch immediately.
In case of cargo spill (hazardous materials): follow ADR procedures, evacuate 50m radius, call HAZMAT team.

4. Temperature-Controlled Cargo
Pharmaceutical shipments: maintain 2-8 degrees Celsius at all times.
Frozen food: maintain -18 degrees Celsius or below.
Any temperature deviation exceeding 2 degrees triggers an automatic alert to dispatch and the quality team.

5. Route Compliance
All drivers must follow approved routes. Deviations require dispatcher approval. GPS tracking is active at all times for fleet monitoring and customer visibility.
