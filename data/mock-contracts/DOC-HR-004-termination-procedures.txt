Employee Termination Procedures — HR CONFIDENTIAL
LogiCore Transport GmbH — HR Department

1. Grounds for Termination
1.1 Performance-based: Two consecutive quarterly reviews below 2.0/5.0 after documented improvement plan.
1.2 Conduct-based: Violation of company policies (see Employee Handbook Section 12).
1.3 Redundancy: Business restructuring approved by the Board.

2. Notice Periods (German Labor Law)
- Probation period (6 months): 2 weeks notice
- Up to 2 years: 4 weeks to the 15th or end of month
- 2-5 years: 1 month to end of month
- 5-8 years: 2 months to end of month
- 8-10 years: 3 months to end of month
- 10-12 years: 4 months to end of month
- 12-15 years: 5 months to end of month
- 15-20 years: 6 months to end of month
- 20+ years: 7 months to end of month

3. Severance Calculations
Standard formula: 0.5 months salary per year of service.
Minimum severance: EUR 5,000 (for employees with 2+ years).
Maximum severance: 18 months salary (capped by social plan agreement with Works Council).

4. Exit Process
- HR conducts exit interview
- IT revokes all system access within 4 hours of notification
- Company property returned (laptop, phone, access cards, company vehicle)
- Final salary payment includes accrued vacation days
- Reference letter provided within 14 days (German law requirement)

5. Works Council Notification
Works Council (Betriebsrat) must be notified before any termination per BetrVG Section 102.
Failure to consult renders the termination void.
